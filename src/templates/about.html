About VoicePress

VoicePress is a beginner-friendly multi-user blogging platform built with Flask and SQLite.

You can draft privately, publish publicly, organize posts with tags and categories, and even use free browser dictation while writing.

Public readers can discover authors, browse recent and popular posts, and join conversations through comments.
